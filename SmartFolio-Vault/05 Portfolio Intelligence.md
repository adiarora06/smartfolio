# Portfolio Intelligence

## Purpose

Portfolio Intelligence connects a user's profile, holdings, risk score, and stock analysis into one decision-support workflow.

## Current Analytics

SmartFolio calculates:

- portfolio value
- risk profile
- current allocation
- target allocation
- allocation gaps
- individual stock concentration
- sector concentration
- one-year scenario return
- target allocation scenario return

## Risk Profiles

- Conservative
- Balanced
- Growth
- Aggressive

## Recommendation Examples

- Increase international equity exposure.
- Increase bond exposure.
- Reduce US equity concentration.
- Reduce single-stock dependency.
- Connect brokerage source for live holdings.

## Important Product Rule

SmartFolio should not say:

> Buy this stock.

SmartFolio should say:

> Here is how this stock or allocation affects your risk, diversification, and goals.
